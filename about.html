We are Audura, a brand provided and supported by Emotive, one of the leading European suppliers of car parts for the automotive aftermarket, trusted in more than 90 countries worldwide, and used by more than 100.000 workshops.

Audura was born in 2016 in Germany to fit the needs of mechanics. Our quality specialists ensure the high quality of our product.

Our parts are manufactured in ISO-certified factories with many years of experience in the production of automotive spare parts.

We approach our work with your satisfaction in first place. For us, Audura is all about providing the best possible experience with a full range of quality parts so that you can go back to the road safely and enjoy your ride.

We simply do everything we can, to keep your car running smoothly.
